The project requires adherence to legal and administrative regulations.  Consultations with legal and engineering experts are needed to ensure compliance and secure necessary permits.  A Planning Permit is required for project plan and design approval.  Design Approval necessitates certified architectural and engineering designs meeting building codes and environmental standards.  A Building Permit is needed for plan approval and adherence to building regulations.  Construction includes periodic quality inspections to ensure compliance.  Property verification confirms legal ownership and registration.  Settlement of potential property rights disputes is necessary.  An environmental impact assessment may be required for some projects.  Certain commercial/industrial projects require specific licenses.  Safety and public health regulations must be met.  Permit and licensing fees are payable according to local regulations.  Development taxes may apply.  Land registry registration is required upon completion.  A compliance certificate confirms construction meets requirements.  If the project involves land subdivision, approvals are needed.
