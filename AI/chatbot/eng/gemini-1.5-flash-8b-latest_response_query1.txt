The project will adhere to legal and administrative requirements.  Legal and engineering experts will be consulted.  A planning permit will be obtained for project plan and design approval.  Architectural and engineering designs will be submitted for approval, meeting building codes and environmental standards.  A building permit will follow design approval, adhering to building regulations.  Periodic inspections will occur during construction to ensure compliance.  Legal ownership and registration of the land will be verified.  Any joint ownership or property disputes will be resolved.  An environmental impact assessment might be required.  Special licenses may be needed for certain projects.  Safety and public health regulations will be met.  Permit and licensing fees, and development taxes will be paid.  The property will be registered in the land registry upon completion.  A compliance certificate will be obtained from local authorities.  Land subdivision approvals will be sought if necessary.
