To develop real estate in Tunisia, you'll need the following permits and authorizations:

*   **Urban Planning Permit:** This is required from local authorities and includes approval of the project plan and design.
*   **Design Approval:** Architectural and engineering plans, certified by licensed professionals, must be submitted for approval.
*   **Building Permit:** After project approval, a building permit must be obtained from the municipality or relevant authority.
*   **Special Licenses:** Certain projects might require specific licenses from relevant organizations, such as the Ministry of Industry or Commerce.
*   **Land Subdivision Authorizations:** If the project involves dividing land into multiple plots, the necessary authorizations must be obtained from the appropriate authorities.
