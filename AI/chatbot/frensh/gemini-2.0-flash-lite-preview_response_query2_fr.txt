To develop real estate in Tunisia, the following permits and authorizations are needed:

*   **Urban Planning Permit:** This is mandatory before any project and is issued by local authorities. It approves the project's plan and design.
*   **Design Approval:** Architectural and engineering projects must be approved, complying with building and environmental standards.
*   **Building Permit:** This must be obtained after project approval. It's issued by the municipality or relevant authority and approves the plans, ensuring compliance with construction regulations.
*   **Special Licenses:** These are required for certain commercial or industrial projects and are obtained from the relevant organizations.
*   **Environmental Impact Assessment:** An environmental impact study may be required for specific projects.
*   **Subdivision Permits:** If the project involves subdividing land, permits are necessary from the relevant authorities.
