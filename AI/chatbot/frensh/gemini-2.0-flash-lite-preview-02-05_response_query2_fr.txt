To develop real estate in Tunisia, you need the following permits and authorizations:

*   **Urban Planning Permit:** Obtain a permit from local authorities before starting the project, including approval of the project plan and design.
*   **Design Approval:** Submit architectural and engineering plans, certified by licensed professionals, for approval.
*   **Building Permit:** Obtain a building permit from the municipality or relevant authority after project approval.
*   **Special Licenses:** Some projects may require specific licenses from relevant organizations, such as the Ministry of Industry or Commerce.
*   **Land Subdivision Authorizations:** If the project involves dividing land into multiple plots, obtain the necessary authorizations from the relevant authorities, including the municipality and the land management office.
