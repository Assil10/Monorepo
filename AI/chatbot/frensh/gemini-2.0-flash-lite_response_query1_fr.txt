Legal requirements for real estate development in Tunisia include:

**Project Planning and Design:**

*   **Urban Planning Permit:** A permit must be obtained from local authorities before starting any project, including approval of the project plan and design in accordance with urban planning laws.
*   **Design Approval:** Architectural and engineering projects must be submitted for approval, with designs conforming to construction and environmental standards.

**Licenses and Construction:**

*   **Building Permit:** After project approval, a building permit must be obtained from the municipality or the relevant authority. This permit includes plan approval and adherence to construction rules.
*   **Quality Controls:** Periodic inspections may be required to ensure compliance with legal standards and specifications.

**Compliance with Land Laws:**

*   **Property Verification:** Ensure the land is legally owned and registered in the land registry.
*   **Property Rights Settlement:** Resolve any co-ownership or dispute issues before development.

**Environmental Standards:**

*   **Environmental Impact Assessment:** For some projects, an environmental impact study may be required and must be approved by environmental authorities.

**Special Licenses and Permits:**

*   **Special Licenses:** Certain projects may require specific licenses from relevant organizations (e.g., Ministry of Industry, Ministry of Commerce).
*   **Safety Standards Compliance:** Adherence to safety and public health regulations.

**Taxes and Fees:**

*   **Permit and License Fees:** Payment of fees related to obtaining permits and licenses.
*   **Development Taxes:** Payment of taxes related to development.

**Property Registration After Development:**

*   **Registration in the Land Registry:** Registration of the property in the land registry once construction is completed.
*   **Certificate of Conformity:** Obtaining a certificate of conformity from local authorities confirming that the construction meets the requirements of the permit and the law.

**Compliance of Land Subdivisions:**

*   **Subdivision:** If the project involves the subdivision of land, obtain the necessary authorizations from the relevant authorities.
