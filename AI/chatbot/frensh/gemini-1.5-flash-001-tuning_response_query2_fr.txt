To develop real estate in Tunisia, you need the following permits and authorizations:

* **Urban planning permit:** This permit is required before starting any real estate development project. It includes the approval of the project's plan and design in accordance with urban planning laws.
* **Design approval:** Architectural and engineering projects, certified by licensed professionals, must be submitted for approval. The designs must comply with construction standards, codes, and environmental regulations.
* **Building permit:** After the project is approved, a building permit must be obtained from the municipality or the relevant authority. This permit includes the approval of the plans and adherence to construction regulations.
* **Special licenses:** Certain projects, such as commercial or industrial developments, may require specific licenses from relevant bodies, such as the Ministry of Industry or the Ministry of Commerce.
* **Subdivision:** If the project involves subdividing a piece of land into multiple plots, the necessary authorizations must be obtained from the relevant authorities, including the municipality and the land management office.
