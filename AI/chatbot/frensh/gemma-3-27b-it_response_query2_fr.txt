To develop real estate in Tunisia, the following permits and authorizations are required:

*   **Planning permission:** This must be obtained from local authorities before starting any project, including approval of the plan and design in accordance with urban planning laws.
*   **Design approval:** Architectural and engineering projects, certified by accredited professionals, must be approved and comply with construction and environmental standards.
*   **Building permit:** This is obtained from the municipality or competent authority after project approval, including plan approval and adherence to building regulations.
*   **Special licenses:** Certain projects (commercial or industrial) may require specific licenses from organizations such as the Ministry of Industry or Commerce.
*   **Subdivision authorizations:** If the project involves dividing land, authorizations are needed from the municipality and the land management office.
*   **Certificate of conformity:** This is obtained from local authorities after construction to confirm compliance with legal requirements.
