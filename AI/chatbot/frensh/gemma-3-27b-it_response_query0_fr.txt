Real estate development in Tunisia requires adherence to a series of legal and administrative requirements designed to regulate the process and ensure compliance with local laws. Developers should consult legal and technical experts to ensure all requirements are met and necessary permits are obtained, helping to avoid legal issues and expedite development.

Specific requirements include:

**Project Planning and Design:**
*   **Urban Planning Permit:** Required before starting any project, involving approval of the plan and design in accordance with urban planning laws.
*   **Design Approval:** Architectural and engineering projects, certified by accredited professionals, must be approved and conform to construction and environmental standards.

**Licensing and Construction:**
*   **Building Permit:** Obtained from the municipality or competent authority after project approval.
*   **Quality Control:** Periodic inspections to ensure compliance with legal standards and specifications.

**Land Law Compliance:**
*   **Property Verification:** The land must be legally owned and registered in the land registry.
*   **Property Rights Resolution:** Disputed property rights must be resolved before development.

**Environmental Standards:**
*   **Environmental Impact Assessment:** May be required for certain projects to assess their impact on the local environment.

**Special Licenses and Permits:**
*   **Special Licenses:** Some projects may require specific licenses from relevant authorities (Ministry of Industry, Ministry of Commerce).
*   **Safety Standards Compliance:** Adherence to public safety and health regulations (security and fire protection systems).

**Taxes and Fees:**
*   **Permit and License Fees:** Payment of fees in accordance with local regulations.
*   **Development Taxes:** Improvement fees or land taxes.

**Property Registration After Development:**
*   **Land Registry Registration:** Once construction is complete, the property must be registered.
*   **Certificate of Conformity:** Confirmation that the construction meets legal requirements.

**Land Subdivision Compliance:**
*   **Subdivision Authorization:** Obtaining necessary permits for land subdivision.
