To develop real estate in Tunisia, you need:

* **A building permit** for approval of the project plan and design.
* **Approval of the design** of architectural and engineering projects, certified by licensed professionals.
* **A construction permit** after project approval, including plan approval and adherence to construction regulations.
* **Verification of land ownership**, ensuring it is legally held and registered with the land registry.  Resolution of any potentially contested property rights is required.
* **An environmental impact assessment** for some projects, requiring approval from environmental authorities.
* **Specific licenses** for certain projects (commercial or industrial) from relevant bodies (Ministry of Industry, Commerce, etc.).
* **Compliance with public health and safety standards**.
