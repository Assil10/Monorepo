To develop real estate in Tunisia, you need the following permits and authorizations:

* **Urban planning permit:** Obtained from local authorities before starting the project, it approves the project's plan and design according to urban planning laws.
* **Design approval:** Architectural and engineering plans, certified by approved professionals, must be approved and comply with construction standards, codes, and environmental regulations.
* **Building permit:** Obtained from the municipality or competent authority after project approval, it approves the plans and ensures compliance with building regulations.
* **Special licenses:** Required for certain projects (commercial or industrial) from competent bodies such as the Ministry of Industry or Commerce.
* **Subdivision authorizations:** If the project involves subdividing land, authorizations are needed from the municipality and the land management office.

Periodic quality control checks may also be required during construction to ensure compliance with legal standards and specifications.
