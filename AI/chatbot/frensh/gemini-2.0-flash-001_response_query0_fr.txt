Real estate development in Tunisia requires adhering to legal and administrative requirements designed to regulate the process and ensure compliance with local laws. Developers should consult legal and technical experts to guarantee all requirements are met and necessary permits are obtained, which helps avoid legal issues and expedite development.

**Project Planning and Design:**

*   **Urban Planning Permit:** Required before starting any project; includes plan and design approval.
*   **Design Approval:** Architectural and engineering projects must be certified by accredited professionals and comply with construction and environmental standards.

**Licenses and Construction:**

*   **Building Permit:** To be obtained after project approval; includes plan approval and adherence to building regulations.
*   **Quality Controls:** Periodic inspections during construction to guarantee compliance with legal standards and specifications.

**Land Law Compliance:**

*   **Property Verification:** Ensuring the land is legally owned and registered in the land registry.
*   **Settlement of Property Rights:** Resolving co-ownership issues or disputes before starting development.

**Environmental Standards:**

*   **Environmental Impact Assessment:** May be required for some projects, necessitating approval from environmental authorities.

**Special Licenses and Permits:**

*   **Special Licenses:** May be necessary for certain projects (commercial, industrial) from relevant agencies.
*   **Compliance with Safety Standards:** Adherence to safety and public health rules, including the installation of safety and fire protection systems.

**Taxes and Fees:**

*   **Permit and License Fees:** Must be paid in accordance with local regulations.
*   **Development Taxes:** May include improvement fees or property taxes.

**Property Registration After Development:**

*   **Registration in the Land Registry:** Necessary once construction is complete, with documents proving compliance with legal specifications.
*   **Certificate of Conformity:** To be obtained from local authorities, confirming the construction meets permit and legal requirements.

**Compliance of Land Subdivisions:**

*   **Subdivision:** Authorizations required if the project involves dividing land into multiple parcels.
