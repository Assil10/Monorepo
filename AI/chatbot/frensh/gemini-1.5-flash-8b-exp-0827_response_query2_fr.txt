To develop real estate in Tunisia, you need:

* **Urban planning permit:** for approval of the project plan and design.
* **Design approval:** of architectural and engineering projects, certified by authorized professionals.
* **Building permit:** after project approval, for approval of plans and compliance with construction regulations.
* **Property verification:** regarding the legality of the land ownership.
* **Resolution of property rights:** if co-ownership or disputes exist.
* **Environmental impact assessment:** (for some projects).
* **Special licenses:** for certain projects like commercial or industrial developments.
* **Compliance with safety standards.**
* **Registration in the land registry:** after construction.
* **Certificate of compliance:** to confirm compliance with the law.
* **Necessary authorizations:** for land subdivision, if the project involves dividing a plot of land.
