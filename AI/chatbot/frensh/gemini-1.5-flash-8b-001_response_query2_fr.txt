To develop real estate in Tunisia, you need:

* **Urban planning permit:** for approval of the project plan and design.
* **Design approval:** for architectural and engineering projects, certified by authorized professionals.
* **Building permit:** after project approval, for approval of the plans and compliance with construction regulations.
* **Property verification:** to ensure the land is legally owned.
* **Resolution of property rights:** in case of co-ownership or disputes.
* **Environmental impact assessment:** (for some projects).
* **Special licenses:** (for some commercial or industrial projects).
* **Necessary authorizations:** for land subdivision, if the project involves dividing a plot of land.
