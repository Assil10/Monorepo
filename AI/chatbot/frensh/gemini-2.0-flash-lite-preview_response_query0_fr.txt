Legal requirements for real estate development in Tunisia include:

*   **Project Planning and Design:**
    *   Urban Planning Permit: Obtaining a permit from local authorities, including approval of the project plan and design.
    *   Design Approval: Submission and approval of architectural and engineering projects, complying with construction and environmental standards.
*   **Licenses and Construction:**
    *   Building Permit: Obtaining a building permit from the municipality, including plan approval and adherence to construction regulations.
    *   Quality Controls: Periodic inspections to ensure compliance with legal standards and specifications.
*   **Compliance with Land Laws:**
    *   Property Verification: Ensuring the land is legally owned and registered in the land registry.
    *   Property Rights Settlement: Resolving property rights in case of co-ownership or disputes.
*   **Environmental Standards:**
    *   Environmental Impact Assessment: An environmental impact study may be required for certain projects.
*   **Special Licenses and Permits:**
    *   Special Licenses: Obtaining specific licenses for certain commercial or industrial projects.
    *   Compliance with Safety Standards: Adherence to safety and public health regulations.
*   **Taxes and Fees:**
    *   Permit and License Fees: Payment of fees related to obtaining permits and licenses.
    *   Development Taxes: Payment of taxes or fees related to development.
*   **Property Registration After Development:**
    *   Registration in the Land Registry: Property registration after construction, with submission of documents proving legal compliance.
    *   Certificate of Conformity: Obtaining a certificate of conformity confirming compliance with permit requirements and the law.
*   **Compliance of Land Subdivisions:**
    *   Subdivision: Obtaining the necessary authorizations for the subdivision of land into multiple parcels.

Developers must consult legal and technical experts to ensure all requirements are met and the necessary permits are obtained.
