The required licenses and approvals for real estate investment in Tunisia are:

- Planning Permit from local authorities, which approves the project plan and design according to urban planning laws.
- Design Approval for architectural and engineering designs certified by licensed professionals.
- Building Permit from the municipality or relevant authority, approving plans and adherence to building regulations.
- Special licenses from relevant bodies like the Ministry of Industry or Ministry of Commerce for commercial or industrial projects.
- Approval from environmental authorities following an Environmental Impact Assessment for specific projects.
- Land Subdivision Approvals from relevant entities, including the municipality and the Land Administration Office, if the project involves land subdivision.
- Certificate of Conformity from local authorities after construction completion.
- Government Approval to purchase land.
