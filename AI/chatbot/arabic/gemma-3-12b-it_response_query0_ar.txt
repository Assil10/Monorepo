Developing real estate in Tunisia requires adherence to a range of legal and administrative requirements intended to regulate the process and ensure compliance with local laws. Developers should consult with legal and engineering experts to guarantee all requirements are met and necessary permits are obtained. Compliance helps avoid legal issues and accelerates development. Key requirements include: planning permits, design approvals, building permits, quality inspections, property ownership verification, property rights settlement, environmental impact assessments, special licenses, safety compliance, permit and license fees, development taxes, land registry registration, a certificate of compliance, and land subdivision.
