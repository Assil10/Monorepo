Planning permit:  Local authorities must approve any real estate development project.
Design approval:  Approved architectural and engineering designs from licensed professionals are required.
Building permit:  A building permit from the municipality or relevant authority is needed after design approval.
Environmental impact assessment:  Some projects may require an environmental impact assessment.
Specific permits:  Certain projects, like commercial or industrial ones, may need specific permits.
Government approval:  Government approval is critical for securing land purchase in Tunisia.
