Planning permit:  Local authorities require a permit before starting any real estate development project.
Design approval:  Approved architectural and engineering designs from licensed professionals must be submitted for approval.
Building permit:  After design approval, a building permit from the municipality or relevant authority is needed.
Environmental impact assessment:  Some projects may require an environmental impact assessment.
Specific licenses:  Certain projects, such as commercial or industrial ones, might require specific licenses.
Government approval:  Government approval is crucial for securing land purchase.
